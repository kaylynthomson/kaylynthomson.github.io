Taylor 312ce Grand Concert V-Class

$1700

Description

The 312ce combines the portable feel of the Grand Concert body with the enhanced sonic power of V-Class bracing. Solid sapele back and sides bring warmth and balance, while a Sitka spruce top adds sparkle and clarity in the treble range. Under the hood, Taylor's new bracing scheme helps this guitar produce surprising volume and incredible sustain with a remarkable soft-touch response, making the 312ce a perfect choice for strummers and fingerpickers alike.

Specifications

  • Scale Length: 24-7/8"
  • Body Width: 15"
  • Body Length: 19 1/2"
  • Body Depth: 4 3/8"
  • Number of Frets: 20
  • Bracing: V-Class Bracing
  • Pickguard: Black
  • Tuners: Taylor Nickel
  • Strings: Phosphor Bronze Light
  • Nut & Saddle: Black Graphite, Micarta saddle
  • Truss Rod Cover: Black Plastic
  • Case: Taylor Deluxe Hardshell Brown
